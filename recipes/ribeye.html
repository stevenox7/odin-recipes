How to prepare the perfect Ribeye

Description

This delicious, mouthwatering, and medium rare ribeye will have your guests begging for more!

Ingredients

  • 16 Oz USDA Prime Ribeye
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • 4 Tbs of Butter
  • 1 Garlic Clove

Steps

  1. Set pan to medium-high heat
  2. While you wait for the pan to heat, salt and pepper the ribeye to your preference. Don't stop until you can see the seasoning!
  3. Add one tablespoon of butter to pan.
  4. Add in the steak to the pan.
  5. Flip the steak every minute for a total of 6 minutes. This ensures even cooking!
  6. At the end of the sixth minute, turn the heat to low and add the remaining Butter and garlic clove.
  7. Using a spoon, base the extra fat on to the top of your steak.
  8. When you're finished, let the steak rest for 10 minutes then enjoy!
